termp shows what you're running in your terminal as Discord Rich Presence — like "playing a game", but it's Claude Code, vim, or lazygit. 48 tools detected out of the box, custom ones via config. Logo, elapsed timer, optional cwd.
├─ a small Go daemon scans your processes ├─ matches them against a tool registry (config.toml, hot reload) ├─ picks one headliner, lists the rest as "also:" └─ sends the payload to Discord over local IPC no bot token · no telemetry · nothing leaves your machine
